About Change Life Private HomeCare

Personal and companion care can benefit your loved one by improving their quality of life.Whether they can no longer drive themselves, need assistance with daily chores, a friendly face to provide companionship, or require more specialized support with illnesses and chronic conditions, Changing Life Private Home Care can help.
Our team consists of compassionate, trustworthy, and certified home aides and caregivers who can offer the personalized care needed to allow your loved one to stay at home.

What makes Change Life different?
At Changing Life Private Home Care, we have seen firsthand the positive outcomes that dedicated home care can bring. Those outcomes drive us to provide top-notch care for your family at affordable rates. Whether your loved one needs long-term care, around-the-clock care, or just occasional companionship, we’re here to help!

When choosing an in-home caregiver for a loved one, we know that dependability and trust are of the utmost importance. At Changing Life Private Home Care, caring for others isn’t just our profession. It’s our passion, and we pride ourselves on maintaining extremely high standards for hiring our caregivers.
Mrs. Sarah Esi Grant
Miss Sarah Esi Grant, the owner of Change Life Private Home Care, an American citizen, immigrate from West Africa(Ghana). She is a Registered Nurse who has a master’s degree in Nursing(MSN), a Master's degree in Business Administration(MBA), a candidate in Doctor in Nurse Practice(DNP). A former Director of Nursing (West Side House) and an Interim DON(Blaire House of Worcester. The owner specialized in wound care, tracheostomy, G-tubes, Peg tubes, and Intravenous medication administration. A dedicated and hard worker who is available for her clients.

According to Sarah,

“Staying home, whether home is a personal residence or an assisted living facility, is the preferred choice of the vast majority of seniors. I have seen it work beautifully for our clients and their families and I am proud to be a part of that solution.”
